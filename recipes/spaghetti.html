Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a pretty good food, it has delicious sauce. It also has meatballs if you want. Super Mario in Real Life.

Ingredients

  • Raw Spaghetti
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Beef, or like pre made meatballs

Steps

  1. Boil the raw Spaghetti pasta in a pot
  2. Once pasta is cooked, add the tomato sauce
  3. And then add the meatballs
