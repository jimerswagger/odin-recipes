Milanesa

Description

Milanesa De Res, which is basically just a fried beef covered in bread crumbs, is one of my favorite foods. My parents spent almost a decade in Argentina and were exposed to this delicious dish. The recipe is not hard to do, although getting the specific ingredients used in Argentina. However, even if the ingredients are not super authentic, the dish still should taste great.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Avocado Oil

Steps

  1. Get a wide container
  2. Spread Bread Crumbs inside the container, covering the floor of it
  3. Place Beef Pieces inside the container and coat each piece completely with bread crumbs
  4. Put Avocado oil inside a frying pan
  5. Set heat on stove to a high temperature where the pan is
  6. Place bread crumb covered beef pieces onto frying pan and fry them each for a few minutes
