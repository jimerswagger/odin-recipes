Hamburger

Description

A classic food and an American staple due to its simple yet delicious makeup. What goes into a Hamburger is up to the one making it, but there absolutely has to be a beef patty and two buns. Afterwards, people can choose to have onions, lettuce and tomatoes in the space in between the buns and the patty.

Ingredients

  • Sesame Seed Buns
  • Raw Beef
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato Slices

Steps

  1. Grill the raw beef
  2. Once cooked, take out two buns and place the beef patty inside
  3. Add Lettuce, Tomato or whatever else you like inside.
